About e-Logbook

e-Logbook is a departure from the traditional way of IT reporting by bringing/closing the gap between the students and their supervisors. Many IT students ususally needed more attention than is given to them during their industrial training resulting in lukewarm attitude on the part of students and a defeat on the aim of the IT in general. This problem may be correted by looking for a medium of continuous interaction between the supervisor and his/her students.

Instructions

1. This book is to assist students to keep record of the training. It will show the departments and section which the student has worked and the period of time spent in each.
2. The daily week carried out during the period of training is to recorded clearly with sketches and diagram where applicable.
3. Students are required to present the log book at intervals to the industry based supervisor for assessment for content and progress.
The supervisor can use any page for his comments where necasssary.
4. The training coordinator of the institution will check the log-books, at regular intervals. This is to ensure that the proper training is being recieved by the student. His comments are to be made on the pages provided by this purpose.
5. The ITF may use the last two pages for it's official purposes.
